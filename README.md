# Overview

ShopSmart is a data-driven application designed to help users reduce the time and effort required to find the cheapest groceries. In today’s world, users often need to manually compare prices across multiple stores or platforms, which is inefficient, repetitive, and frustrating. This project addresses that problem by allowing users to input a list of grocery items and automatically aggregating and analyzing product pricing data from different sources to determine the most cost-effective store for the entire basket. By building a pipeline that collects, cleans, stores, and processes pricing data, ShopSmart enables users to receive data-informed recommendations that optimize their shopping decisions and help them save both time and money.

> Story

_On a Saturday morning, Anna opens her phone while sitting at her kitchen table, planning her weekly groceries. She has a fixed budget this_ _month and wants to be more careful with her spending. Normally, she would open multiple store apps and websites, switching between tabs, trying_ _to remember prices and mentally calculate totals. After a few minutes, she usually feels overwhelmed and unsure whether she is actually saving money or just spending time._

_This time, she uses ShopSmart. She types in her grocery list: milk, eggs, bread, chicken, rice, and vegetables. Within seconds, the app processes her list and compares prices across different stores. Instead of manually checking each item, Anna sees a clear breakdown:_ _total cost per store, item-by-item comparisons, and suggestions for where she can buy everything at the lowest overall price._

_One store stands out as the most cost-efficient option. Another store is slightly more expensive but offers better prices for certain items._ _Anna quickly understands her trade-offs without needing to do any manual calculations. In less than a minute, she makes a decision and heads_ _out to shop, confident that she is not overspending._

_What used to take her 20–30 minutes of comparing and second-guessing now takes just a few moments. For Anna, ShopSmart doesn’t just save money—it removes friction from a routine task and gives her clarity and confidence in her decisions._
